Founded by IBDP Graduates

Arthashala was born from the passion and vision of Nepali International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme graduates who recognized the critical gap in financial education within Nepal's youth community.

Having experienced world-class education ourselves, we understand the transformative power of knowledge. We're committed to bringing that same standard of financial literacy education to every corner of Nepal, making it accessible, engaging, and culturally relevant.

Our team combines international educational standards with deep understanding of Nepal's unique economic landscape, ensuring our programs are both globally informed and locally applicable.
