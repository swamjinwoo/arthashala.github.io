Our Story

Building Financial Literacy in Nepal

Who We Are

Our Vision

Arthashala envisions a Nepal where every young person possesses the financial knowledge and confidence to navigate the modern economy, contribute to national prosperity, and achieve personal financial freedom.

Our Mission

To democratize financial education in Nepal by providing accessible, culturally relevant financial literacy programs that empower young minds to make informed economic decisions and build sustainable wealth.

Founded by IBDP Graduates

Our team combines international educational standards with a deep understanding of Nepal's unique economic landscape. We bring world-class financial education to every corner of Nepal, making it accessible, engaging, and culturally relevant.
